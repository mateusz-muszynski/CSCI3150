Our Mission

To establish an inclusive, accessible, and innovative arts center that nurtures creativity, strengthens community bonds, and empowers individuals to experience, create, and connect.

Our Vision

A strong, connected, and vibrant community driven by engagement with the arts—a home for artists and arts organizations to engage with community together. A safe space where everyone is welcome and creativity unites us.

Our Story

Behind this effort is a passionate group of community leaders and artists working hard to make this dream a reality. Our journey began in 2010 with the formation of ARTS RWC, a collective of artists, art advocates, organizations, and civic leaders dedicated to advancing Redwood City as a dynamic and sustainable arts community.
