My Favorite Show: “The Bear”

As a Chicago native, I’m passionate about cooking, and watching The Bear has been a huge inspiration in my own kitchen adventures. This critically acclaimed series follows Chef Carmy’s journey through the city’s vibrant culinary scene, and it’s based right here in my hometown of Chicago. The show has won multiple awards, including an Emmy due to its authentic storytelling and outstanding performances. Learn more on the official Hulu page or read a review on Rotten Tomatoes .
